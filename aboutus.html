Our club

2011 - Present

The Baruch Esports Association was founded by League of
Legends fanatics. Eventually we started branching into other
major titles, like Starcraft and CSGO. In the current day, we
operate both comepitively and casually. We host a space on
campus for casual gamers, with bi-weekly events.

We also operate competitively, having teams in VALORANT,
Legends of Legends, Overwatch 2, iRacing, Mario Kart and
possibly even more.

Our club welcomes gamers of any level!
