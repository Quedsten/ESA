About Us

Our Activities

Online Events

Every month, we host online events to bring out the community together. If you can't make it in-person because of classes, we have a diverse and eager community looking to play games competitively and casually. We host online tournaments and game nights every month

In-Person Events

We host bi-weekly in-person events in the Lexington Building in room 12-02. These events start at 12:40PM and last until 2:30PM. The events we have in-person lean towards the casual side, with games such as Jenga, Pick Your Poison, craftinf events, and more. You can sometimes catch us hosting competetive events, such as Smash.

Competitive

Our competitive teams compete in a variety of leagues, such as CVAL, CLoL, PayFly, and so much more. They host tryouts every semester and host scrimmages weekly.
